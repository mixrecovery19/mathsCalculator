Calculating the Determinant of 2 x 2 Matrix

When you click "Generate 2 x 2 Determinant" your initial 2 x 2 matrix will be used to calculate the determinant.
The determinant of a 2 x 2 matrix is calculated as (ad - bc), where a, b, c, and d are the elements of the matrix.
This allows you to easily calculate the determinant working through the 1/ad-bc formula.

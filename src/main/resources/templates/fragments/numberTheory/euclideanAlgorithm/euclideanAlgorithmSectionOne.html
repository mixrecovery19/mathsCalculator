Step-by-Step Euclidean Algorithm Walkthrough

About this walkthrough

The Euclidean Algorithm repeatedly divides the larger number by the smaller number.

After each division, the remainder becomes the new divisor and the process repeats.

Once the remainder becomes zero, the last non-zero remainder is the Greatest Common Divisor (GCD).

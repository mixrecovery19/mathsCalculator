Step Process To Calculate the Inverse of A

Now that the determinant has been calculated, we can continue to calculate the inverse of the \(2 \times 2\) matrix.

Given the matrix:\[A=\begin{bmatrix}a & b \\c & d\end{bmatrix}\] the inverse is calculated using: \[A^{-1}=\frac{1}{\det(A)}\begin{bmatrix}d & -b \\-c & a\end{bmatrix}\] This walkthrough first swaps the main diagonal values, then changes the signs of the off-diagonal values, then applies the determinant. This allows you to calculate the inverse as a next step for the linear systems wlakthrough, and see the process step by step.

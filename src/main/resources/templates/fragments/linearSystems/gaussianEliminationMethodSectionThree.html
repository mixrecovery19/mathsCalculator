The updated augmented matrix is \[\left[\begin{array}{cc|c} a&b&c\\ 0&e'&f' \end{array} \right] \] Since the coefficient of \(x\) is now zero, the second row represents \[e'y=f'\] Dividing both sides by \(e'\) gives \[y=\frac{f'}{e'}\]

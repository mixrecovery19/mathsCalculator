Calculating the Determinant of a 2 x 2 Matrix

The determinant helps determine whether a matrix can be inverted.
The first step of calculating the inverse of any matrix is to calculate the determinant.
Unlike the 3 x 3 determinat, the 2 x 2 determinant can be calculated using a simple formula.
For a 2 x 2 matrix the determinant is calculated as (ad - bc).
If the determinant is zero, the matrix is singular and does not have an inverse.
If the determinant is non-zero you can proceed to calculate the inverse. Enter your values, press Create Matrix to see a step by step walkthrough of how to calculate a 2 x 2 determinant.

2. Construct the Matrix Equation

Building the Matrix Representation

Your two simultaneous equations can now be written as a matrix equation.

The left matrix contains the coefficients of x and y, while the column vector on the right contains the constants.

Together they form \[A X = B\] where \[A=\begin{bmatrix}a & b\\d & e\end{bmatrix} \quad X=\begin{bmatrix}x\\y\end{bmatrix} \quad B=\begin{bmatrix}c\\f\end{bmatrix}\]

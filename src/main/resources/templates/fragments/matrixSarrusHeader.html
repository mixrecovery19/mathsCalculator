Calculating the Determinant of a 3 × 3 Matrix using the Sarrus Method

The determinant helps determine whether a matrix can be inverted.
For a 3 × 3 matrix, the determinant can be calculated using the Sarrus method.
In essence the Sarrus method is a series of elementary mathematics, performed in order allowing to calculate the determinant efficiently manually.
